Many of them seem to have a linear relationship with the SalePrice.
A lot of data points are located on x = 0 which may indicate the absence of such feature in the house.
Therefore we must calculate coefficients again after removing such training points.

In [15]:
df_corr = pd.DataFrame(columns=['col', 'pearson'])
for col in df_num.columns[:-1]:
    coeff = df_num.loc[df_num[col]!=0, [col, 'SalePrice']].corr().iloc[0,1]
    df_corr.loc[df_corr.shape[0]] = [col, coeff]
